The sandy terrain has a way of clogging your gears and weighing you down, forcing you to choose your path with care. Obstacles are not only barriers—they are puzzles that, when overcome, may reveal hidden potions and items to restore your strength. Whether it’s breaking through a wall of weathered stone or clearing the overgrown palms, the cost of progress is steep, and your dwindling durability reminds you that caution is a virtue here.
